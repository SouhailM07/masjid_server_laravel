🧠 Step 1 — Think in real life (not code)

Ask:

👉 “Who depends on who?”

Does:

❓ Role depend on User
or
❓ User depend on Role

In real life:

Role can exist alone (Admin, Editor, User)

User needs a role to define permissions

So:

✅ Role is independent
✅ User depends on Role

🔑 Core rule

The table that stores the foreign key belongsTo the other table.
